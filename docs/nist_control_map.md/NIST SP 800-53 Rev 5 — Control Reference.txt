# NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 — Control Reference

This document maps each check in the Compliance Evidence Collector to its corresponding NIST 800-53 control family.

## Control Families Used in This Tool

### AC — Access Control
| Control | Name | Why It's Checked |
|---|---|---|
| AC-2 | Account Management | Ensure accounts are reviewed, inactive accounts disabled, and access is appropriate |
| AC-3 | Access Enforcement | Enforce approved authorizations for logical access |

### AU — Audit and Accountability
| Control | Name | Why It's Checked |
|---|---|---|
| AU-2 | Audit Events | Systems must log security-relevant events |
| AU-9 | Protection of Audit Information | Logs must be protected from tampering or deletion |

### CM — Configuration Management
| Control | Name | Why It's Checked |
|---|---|---|
| CM-6 | Configuration Settings | Systems must follow documented secure baselines |
| CM-7 | Least Functionality | Only necessary services, ports, and protocols should be enabled |

### IA — Identification and Authentication
| Control | Name | Why It's Checked |
|---|---|---|
| IA-2 | Identification and Authentication | Users must be uniquely identified; MFA is required for privileged accounts |
| IA-5 | Authenticator Management | Password policies and credential hygiene must be enforced |

### SI — System and Information Integrity
| Control | Name | Why It's Checked |
|---|---|---|
| SI-2 | Flaw Remediation | Systems must be patched within defined timeframes |

## Severity Rating Guide

| Severity | Meaning | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| CRITICAL | Immediate risk | Remediate within 24 hours |
| HIGH | Significant gap | Remediate within 7 days |
| MEDIUM | Control weakness | Remediate within 30 days |
| INFO | Passing check | Document as evidence |
